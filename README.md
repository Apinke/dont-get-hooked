## About

**Don't Get Hooked!** is a three-volume interactive flippable comic series on phishing — the most common and most costly cyberattack in the world. Written by Gbemisola Esho, Google Developer Expert (GDE) and Women Techmakers (WTM) Ambassador, the series makes phishing awareness accessible to every level of reader.

This repository contains **Volume 1 — Beginner Level**, designed for anyone who uses a smartphone or email account. No technical knowledge required.

---

## 📖 What's Inside

| Chapter | Title | What You Learn |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | The Suspicious Message | What phishing is and how urgency is weaponised |
| 2 | The Fake Disguise | How lookalike websites are built and how to spot them |
| 3 | The Email Trap | Email phishing red flags and sender address forensics |
| 4 | Smishing — Text Traps | SMS phishing targeting mobile money and telecom users |
| 5 | Vishing — Voice Scams | Phone call scams and why your bank never asks for your PIN |
| 6 | AI Makes Phishing Scarier | Why grammar mistakes no longer reveal phishing attacks |
| 7 | Passwords & 2FA | Strong passwords and two-factor authentication |
| 8 | If You Get Phished | Emergency response — what to do in the first 5 minutes |
| 9 | AI Fights Back | How AI-powered tools protect you in real time |
| 10 | Your Survival Guide | Amara's 5 rules: Stop · Check · Verify · Protect · Report |
